Digestive System

DIGESTIVE SYSTEM IN HUMANS

Overview

The digestive system is a group of organs that work together to break down food and absorb nutrients for the body's energy needs. It consists of several parts:

  • Mouth
  • Esophagus
  • Stomach
  • Small Intestine
  • Large Intestine
  • Rectum

Function

The digestive system performs the following functions:

  1. Ingestion: Taking in food through the mouth
  2. Digestion: Breaking down food into smaller molecules
  3. Absorption: Absorbing nutrients into the bloodstream
  4. Elimination: Removing waste materials from the body
